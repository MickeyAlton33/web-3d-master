Aperture I

The Abyssal Tide

An infinite ocean suspended in darkness. Waves of deep cerulean fold endlessly upon themselves, governed by forces unseen. Some say if you watch long enough, the tide responds to your presence.

Aperture II

The Convergence

A field of ancient stars collapsing inward toward a singularity that never arrives. The points of light accelerate, spiral, and reform -- an eternal breath of the cosmos caught mid-exhale.

Aperture III

The Veil

Ribbons of spectral light dance across a polar sky that exists in no known hemisphere. Greens bleed into violet, violet into gold. The atmosphere itself appears to breathe.

Aperture IV

The Cradle

A stellar nursery viewed from impossible proximity. Clouds of ionized gas swirl in hues of magenta and amethyst, illuminated from within by stars not yet born.
